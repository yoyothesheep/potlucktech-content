## Company

**Potluck Technologies** is a software development consulting firm founded in 2023 by two former Google engineering and product leaders. They help early-stage tech companies build successful products and high-performing teams.

**Tagline:** "Everyone brings something to the table"

**Mission:** Help early-stage technology companies build successful products and high-performing teams.

**Location:** New York, NY (primary)

**Website:** https://www.potlucktech.com

---

## Services

| Service | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fractional Leadership** | Embed product/engineering leaders to set strategy, manage deliverables, lead teams, build AI stacks |
| **Advisory** | Weekly sessions on product strategy, engineering roadmaps, team performance, technical hiring |
| **Full-Stack Product Builds** | End-to-end builds from concept through PMF — PM, UI/UX, AI integration, software engineering |
| **Coaching** | Executive coaching for ICs (junior–staff), managers, senior leaders, founders, PMs |
