# Case Study: Alt Path / Arete Rising
**Client:** Arete Rising / Alt Path (altpath.org)
**Engagement start:** June 2024
**Status:** V1 draft — needs client review and quotes

---

## The Problem

Alt Path exists to solve a real and measurable crisis: LA County has a 53% workforce misalignment rate — 70% higher than Atlanta — with training credentials concentrated in low-growth fields while high-demand middle-skill roles go unfilled. 19% of county jobs face automation risk. The adults who need career navigation most — first-gen workers, career changers, people without four-year degrees — have the fewest tools to help them find viable paths.

The platform had the right mission. The product wasn't delivering it.

Built on Bubble, altPath was slow and unfocused. 99% of visitors bounced. The core team knew the work mattered but didn't have the product infrastructure — or the team infrastructure — to fix it.

---

## What We Did

Potluck Technologies came on as fractional product and engineering leadership in June 2024.

**On the product side**, we rebuilt altPath from the ground up in custom software. The work wasn't just technical. It required defining what a successful user journey looked like for a first-gen adult navigating career options for the first time — what information they needed, when they needed it, and what "finding something useful" actually meant for this population.

**On the team side**, we restructured how Arete Rising hired and worked with contractors. The core team had been spending on expensive contractors without the vocabulary to direct them or evaluate their output. We built the internal processes, AI workflow tooling, and hiring frameworks that let a lean team run a high-performing product operation — and built the core team's capability to sustain it independently.

---

## Results

**Product**
- Bounce rate: 99% → 35%
- 70% of new accounts save at least one career

**Team**
- Replaced expensive, unfocused contractor setup with a lean, high-performing team
- AI product tooling and internal workflow processes integrated
- Core team now hires and directs contractors independently

---

## Why It Matters

The numbers are meaningful because of who they represent. A first-gen adult who lands on altPath and saves a career isn't just a metric — they're someone who found a path that didn't exist for them before. The platform was always designed for that person. Now it works for them.
