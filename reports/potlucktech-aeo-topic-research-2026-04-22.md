## Content Presentation Recommendations

**1. Open every page with a direct, AI-citeable answer block.**
Format as a boxed or bolded 2–4 sentence "Quick Answer" at the very top, before any introduction. This is the text AI engines pull for citations. 75% of top cited pages do this; Potluck's site currently has none.

**2. Add FAQPage schema to every pillar page.**
Only 2 of 12 crawled competitor pages use FAQPage schema despite it being one of the strongest AI-citation signals. Implement on all 8 Create Now pieces immediately.

**3. Use comparison tables for all vs./or content.**
4 of the top cited pages use tables for fractional vs. full-time comparisons. Tables are highly citeable — AI engines extract structured data. Every comparison piece needs at least one table.

**4. Surface author credentials inline, not just in a bio section.**
Top cited pages include author credentials in the intro: "Written by [Name], former Google engineering leader and fractional CTO to 30+ startups." Place this in the first 100 words of every piece.

**5. End every piece with a "When to talk to Potluck" section, not a generic CTA.**
A self-selection section ("This is right for you if X, Y, Z — and here's when it's not") converts better and builds credibility that earns citations from roundup sites like aikenhouse.com's "Best fractional CTO services" lists.
